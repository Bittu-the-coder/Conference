Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register for the conference?

You can register through our registration page. Early bird registration is available until [date]. Visit our registration section for more details about fees and deadlines.

What are the important dates I should know about?

Key dates include: Paper submission deadline (March 15), Notification of acceptance (April 15), Early bird registration (May 1), and Conference dates (June 15-17).

Is accommodation provided for attendees?

While we don't provide direct accommodation, we have partnered with several hotels near the venue offering special rates for conference attendees. Details can be found in the registration package.

What is the paper submission format?

Papers should be submitted in IEEE format, not exceeding 6 pages. All submissions must be original and unpublished. Please check our guidelines section for detailed formatting instructions.

Will the conference be hybrid or in-person only?

The conference will be conducted in a hybrid mode, allowing both in-person and virtual participation. Virtual attendees will have access to all sessions through our online platform.
