Department of Mathematics & Scientific Computing

History

The Department of Mathematics and Scientific Computing attained its present status of an independent department on 22nd June, 2019. Prior to this department was constituent part of Applied Science Department, established in 1962.

Our Commitment

The department is committed to impart the effective teaching and quality research work in different areas of Mathematics and Scientific Computing.

Programs Offered

  • M.Sc. Mathematics (Specialization in Computing)
  • Ph.D. in Mathematics
  • Mathematics courses for:
    • Undergraduate Engineering students
    • Postgraduate Engineering students
    • Science departments
    • Management departments
